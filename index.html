About CodeCanvas

CodeCanvas is your creative playground to discover, share, and be inspired by beautiful front-end projects from around the world. Whether you're a beginner or a seasoned developer, our open community lets you showcase your designs, learn from others, and grow together. Built with love using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, CodeCanvas is an open-source platform dedicated to collaboration, innovation, and growth.

With features like project discovery, easy submissions, tagging, and upcoming enhancements like user profiles and reviews, we aim to make front-end development more fun, accessible, and interactive. Join us and become part of a global movement to inspire and be inspired!
